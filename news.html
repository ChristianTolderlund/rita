COLOR FOR CLIMATE

EXHIBITION

I have a pleasure to invite you to see my latest project, Colors for Climate - a dual exhibition and a part of the upcoming Nature & Culture - International Poetry Film Festival.

The art will be on exhibited at Red Door Gallery from Oct 29 to Nov 23

The Colors for Climate project aims to portray different parts of the world that deserve our attention. My mission is to take the greatest and most abstract challenges of our time, such as wildlife conservation and climate change, and translate it into something personal and meaningful to every human being.
