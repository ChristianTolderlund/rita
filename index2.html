About Me

My name is Rita Howis. I am a creative rebel and an artist by heart. Originally from Russia, I have lived in 7 countries and now happily settled in Denmark - the hotspot for design and sustainability.

About Colorflow

My mission is to make exclusive pieces of artwork that everybody can relate to. Inspired by nature, I've developed a unique technique using fluid paint materials that mimic our natural environment.

Plant A Tree

I choose to team up with EcoTree and from now on I plant a tree from each produced or sold painting!
